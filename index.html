About Us

Who are we?

HEPTAGON MUSIC is a record label from Argentina with the mission of uniting the "Seven Sides of the World" with MUSIC.

Our artists use their music to share their stories, express their emotions, and connect with others while conveying a positive message.

We believe that music is a force for good in the world. It can bring people together, inspire change, and make a positive impact.

JAE SVN

Artist & Director
